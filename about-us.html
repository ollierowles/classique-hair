10 minutes walk from Oxford station, we are right on the edge of the city centre, with lots of connections via public transport.

Established in 1967, we have been cutting hair in Oxford for over 55 years! A family-run business which prides itself on friendly service and quality cuts and colours. We are a unisex salon, on the outskirts of the centre of Oxford, offering a wide range of services to all hair types.

We have 3 friendly stylists Lisa, Noriko and Denka, who are all experienced stylists

> How we started out

Opened by Hilary in 1967 along Botley Road, we have been along Botley Road for over 55 years. We have many local customers who have been coming to our salon for years. We very much feel apart of the Botley community.
